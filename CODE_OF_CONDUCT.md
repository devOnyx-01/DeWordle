# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We are committed to making participation in DeWordle respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free for everyone.

## Expected Behavior
- Be respectful and constructive in discussions.
- Assume good intent and give actionable feedback.
- Focus critique on ideas and code, not people.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation.
- Personal attacks, doxxing, or hateful language.
- Repeated disruption of project discussions.

## Enforcement
Maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or restrict access for behavior that violates this policy.

## Reporting
Please report concerns privately to project maintainers via security channels or direct contact listed in the repository.
